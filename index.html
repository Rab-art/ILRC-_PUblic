About Us

The Islamic Legal Resource Centre is a group of independent Muslim lawyers in Alberta, specializing in family law. We aim to support the Muslim community by providing culturally sensitive legal resources and services.

Why We Started

We created this centre because we became disheartened by seeing so many members of our community engaged in the family justice system. Our goal is to offer support and prevent unnecessary legal disputes within the community.

Family Law

We offer mediation and arbitration services to help families resolve disputes outside of court, in a culturally sensitive manner. Our goal is to support families pre and post-separation, working with local imams to ensure that separations are handled properly according to Canadian law and Islamic principles.

Early Intervention & Pre-Marriage

We provide counseling and psychological services to assist those facing family law-related challenges. Our early intervention services connect individuals with registered psychologists and other professionals in the hopes of reconciliation and relationship coordination.
